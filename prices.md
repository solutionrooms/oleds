## Panel Type Guide (Important)

| Panel | TVs | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **QD-OLED** | Samsung S95F, S95H, S85F, Sony Bravia 8 II | Quantum Dot + OLED. Saturated colour, very bright. Best overall picture. |
| **Primary RGB Tandem OLED evo** | LG G5, G6, Panasonic Z95B | LG's premium panel. Brightest OLED, best for bright rooms. |
| **Tandem OLED evo** | LG C5 | Good brightness, step below G-series. The C5 is better than the new C6 at 55". |
| **Standard W-OLED** | LG C6 (55"), Samsung S90F/S90H | Standard WOLED. Good contrast but lower brightness. C6 55" is a step down from C5. |
| **OLED SE** | Panasonic Z86C | LG's budget 2026 panel. Removes polariser, lower brightness. Entry-level. |
