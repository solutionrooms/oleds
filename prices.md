| Panel | TVs | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **QD-OLED** | Samsung S95F, S95H, S85F, Sony Bravia 8 II | Quantum Dot + OLED. Saturated colour, very bright. Best overall picture quality. |
| **Primary RGB Tandem OLED evo** | LG G5, G6, Panasonic Z95B | LG's premium panel. Brightest OLED, best for bright rooms. |
