## April 2026

### Samsung S95H Now On Sale in US — UK Launch Imminent
The S95H launched for sale in the US on **2 April 2026**, with confirmed US pricing:
- 55" — $2,499
- 65" — $3,400
- 77" — $4,500
- 83" — $6,500

UK pricing has still not been officially announced, but with the US launch now live, a UK release is expected within weeks. Based on the S95F's launch cadence (US April, UK late April/May), UK pricing could arrive any day. Samsung's 2026 OLED lineup has also been described as launching "globally" from April 2026.

Key upgrades over the S95F: **35% brighter** (up to 3,000 nits peak), new **FloatLayer Design** with metallic bezel, **OLED HDR Pro** panel with Glare Free 3.0, optional HDMI expansion box. The S95H is the first Samsung OLED to feature **Art Mode**.

Note: the 48" and 83" S95H models use LG Tandem W-OLED panels (not QD-OLED), while 55", 65", and 77" retain Samsung's QD-OLED.
